About Me

Hello! I’m Benjamen Centeno, an Environmental Product Design major at CU Boulder with a minor in Creative Technology & Design. I combine sustainable materials, small-batch manufacturing, and hands-on mechanical work to create products with real community impact. Outside of school, I run BoBo’s Moped Repair Shop in Boulder, where I restore and sell vintage mopeds and motorcycles.

Highlighted Project

Recycled Dog Bowls

One of my latest projects includes creating durable dog bowls made from old 5-gallon buckets, frisbee discs, and colorful scraps from a dog toy factory. Each bowl diverts plastic from waste streams while providing a practical, sustainable product for dog owners in Boulder.
